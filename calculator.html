Assumptions & methodology
  • FY2026 base levy: $130.2M (FY2023 levy of $119.1M grown at 3%/yr)
  • FY2026 total assessed value: $11.65B (residential $9.85B, commercial $0.78B)
  • Residential tax rate: $10.67/$1,000 AV; Commercial: $20.93/$1,000 AV (estimated FY2026)
  • Expenditure growth: 6%/yr (FFC estimate, driven by health insurance, SPED, debt service)
  • Revenue growth without override: ~2.5%/yr (Prop 2½ limit; new growth and state aid assumed roughly offsetting)
  • Override permanently raises the levy limit; future growth still capped at 2.5%/yr
  • Assessed value assumed to grow ~4%/yr; tax rate impact calculated on projected AV
  • Beverly has ~15,400 housing units (2020 Census)
  • Tax bill impact calculated on your selected home value using the residential rate increase
