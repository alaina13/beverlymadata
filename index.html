Open Beverly

Beverly faces a projected $7.1M deficit by FY2028. Under Proposition 2½, the property tax levy can grow 2.5% per year. The city's costs, including health insurance, pensions, and debt service, have grown at 6%. The gap between those two rates widens every year.
